What does the Output mean?

The resulting value (in Watt-hours, Wh) represents the highly probable electrical load required by the building's appliances and HVAC systems for the upcoming 10-minute interval. By using cyclical time encoding (Sine/Cosine of the hour) and environmental deltas, the AI recognizes non-linear human behavioral patterns.

Why is this technically significant?

Traditional Building Energy Management Systems (BEMS) are reactive. This framework enables Proactive Control (Peak Shaving). If the Deep ANN predicts a sudden surge in energy demand at 18:00, the BEMS can automatically pre-cool the building at 17:30 or dispatch stored battery power, drastically reducing operational costs and grid stress.
